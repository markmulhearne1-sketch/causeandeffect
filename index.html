cause & effect is built for people who have internalised one truth: every decision in the chain has a consequence, and you are accountable for both. we bring together the most senior supply chain and operations leaders on the planet for candid intelligence sharing, honest benchmarking, and strategic conversations that never leave the room.

signal, not noise.
peer intelligence that moves strategy.

most supply chain executives spend their days surrounded by vendors selling them something. cause & effect is different. it is a closed collective where cscos and senior logistics leaders share real data, real problems, and real solutions — with people who carry exactly the same weight you do.

operation: member intelligence
01 // intelligence
monthly strategic briefings

curated intelligence on freight markets, geopolitical disruption, supplier risk, and technology shifts. written by practitioners, not analysts. no advertorial content. no fluff. just signal.

02 // network
private peer network

a vetted community of cscos, vps of logistics, heads of procurement, and operations directors. direct access to the people solving the same problems you are — at companies that actually operate at scale.

03 // crisis
rapid response network

when a port shuts down at 2am or a key supplier fails overnight, you don't need a consultant — you need someone who has been there. cause & effect members have first-call access to peer expertise in real time.

04 // benchmarking
confidential data exchange

anonymous benchmarking across freight cost, inventory ratios, technology stack, and team structure. understand how your operations compare — without giving anything away publicly.

05 // vendors
pre-vetted introductions

no cold pitches. no wasted time. a vendor only reaches the cause & effect recommended list after peer review. if it makes it through, at least three cscos have already kicked the tyres.

06 // events
closed-door roundtables

intimate gatherings in major logistics hubs — 12 people maximum. structured discussion, no slides, no sponsors in the room. what is said in the room stays in the room.
